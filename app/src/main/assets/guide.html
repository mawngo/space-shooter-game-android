Game Rules

Home | Play

1. Controls

  Keyboard (Recommended):

  • Change the ship direction using the arrow keys.
  • Press s or Left Click to shoot regular bullets.
  • Press a or Right Click to shoot special bullets collected.

  Touchscreen:

  • Swipe Up/Down/Left/Right to change the ship direction.
  • Touch (one finger) to shoot regular bullets.
  • Multitouch (two fingers) to shoot collected special bullets.

2. Bullets

  • Regular bullets:
    • Can be fired freely.
    • Regular bullets have a 60% chance of destroying asteroids :)).
    • Regular bullets bounce back upon collision.
    • Collisions with regular bullets cause damage and a loss of points.
    • Regular bullets obey the laws of physics :)).
  • Special bullets:
    • Limited quantity.
    • Shows the current type in the Ammo display.
    • The effects depend on the type of bullet.
    • Special bullets have a 100% chance of destroying asteroids.

3. Items

  • Burger: Restores health.
  • Money: Adds points.
  • One bullet (red): This bullet destroys asteroids with 100% certainty, bounces back upon hitting walls, and restores health when it hits the player.
  • Three bullets (blue x 3): These bullets destroy asteroids with 100% certainty and do not bounce back upon hitting walls.
  • Swirl (circular bullet): This item destroys all existing asteroids and bullets.

4. Asteroids

  • Asteroids spawn from the central planet, and their quantity increases over time.
  • Collisions with asteroids destroy the asteroids and cause damage to the player.
  • Asteroids can break into smaller fragments, and the damage and points awarded depend on the size of the asteroid.
  • Asteroids bounce back upon collision and follow the laws of physics :D.
